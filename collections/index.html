FairweatherEditions

Fairweather Editions specializes in limited edition, hand made artist books and portfolios.
All of the books that we offer are completely handmade using imported
archival papers, imported book cloth, leathers and end papers.
We sell on approval and guarantee satisfaction on any item purchased from Fairweather Editions.

ONE DOZEN ROSES

A collection of 12 different photos of roses, each printed on Japanese Gampi paper and tipped in by hand. The photos by Cy DeCosse range from single flowers to bouquets and from fresh cut to romantic dried. This hand bound book is made to order and can be “personalized”for a unique and memorable gift for weddings and special occasions.

THE 4 GREEK ELEMENTS

Earth, Air, Fire and Water the classic and ancient elements as photographed by Cy DeCosse. Four books. Each book features twenty original images printed individually on Japanese Gampi paper and mounted by hand. The books are hand bound with imported and archival materials with gold stamping and a unique glass inset cover design. This edition is limited to fifteen sets.

DARK MATTER by Keith Taylor

A conceptual investigation of the visual phenomena of shadows inside the CDMS II lab and in the forest surrounding the site where scientists seek the mysterious missing mass called “dark matter”. This edition of 20 hand pulled photogravures by Keith Taylor comes boxed in a unique clamshell case covered with Canapetta book cloth and handmade Cave papers.
