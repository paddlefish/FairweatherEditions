Quality

The beauty of a hand-made print and its tactile quality cannot be communicated on screen, it must seen and felt, and for that reason we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

How to Order

If you are interested in purchasing a print, click the 'Buy' button next to the appropriate image and complete the online contact form. We will respond via email within 24 hours with the current edition number and an invoice.

Payment

We accept payment using PayPal or by check. If payment is made by check, then the print will be shipped upon clearing. Shipping charges are NOT included and will be added to the price. We ship using FedEx. Minnesota addresses will be charged the appropriate sales tax.

Guarantee and Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Therefore, if you are not satisfied (for whatever reason) when you receive your print, you may return it within 7 days, in the original shipping container and in the same condition as you received it, and your purchase price (less shipping) will be refunded. No questions asked.
